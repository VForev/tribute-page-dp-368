Bobby Enslow

The Founder of INDABA COFFEE

Bobby Enslow in front of Indaba Broadway, circa 2015

Timeline

2009 - Indaba opened in West Central in fall of 2009 with “just an espresso machine, a couple of grinders, and like $10,000 in startup capital,” the business has grown steadily, first serving local coffee, and then going to a hip, multi-roaster format.

2014 - Indaba Coffee began roasting their own beans.

2015 - Indaba Coffee opened there second location.

2017 - Indaba Coffee opened there third location. Summit

2018 - Indaba Coffee opened there forth location. Riverside

2024 - Indaba Coffee opened there fifth location. North Monroe

“Coffee is relational, it’s communal. You see people change, struggle and turn it around.” -- Bobby Enslow
